Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2025 | Developer: ROHOR studio

1. Overview

YouTube Smart Notifications ("the App") is developed by ROHOR studio. This Privacy Policy explains what data the App accesses, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

2. Notification Access (Notification Listener Permission)

The App requires the Notification Listener Service permission (android.permission.BIND_NOTIFICATION_LISTENER_SERVICE).

This permission is used solely to:

  • Detect when a new YouTube video upload notification arrives on your device.
  • Extract the video title and channel name from that notification in order to find and summarize the video.

We do not read, store, transmit, or process notifications from any other application. Only notifications originating from the official YouTube app are processed. No notification content is ever sent to any third-party server other than the AI summarization service you have explicitly configured (Google Gemini).

3. Data We Collect and Process

The App processes the following data locally on your device:

  • YouTube notification content — video title and channel name, used only to identify the video for summarization.
  • Video summaries — AI-generated summaries are stored locally in an on-device database (Room/SQLite).
  • API keys — Your Google Gemini API key is stored securely on-device using Android's EncryptedSharedPreferences. It is never transmitted anywhere except directly to the respective AI provider's API.
  • Channel subscriptions — The list of YouTube channels you add to the App is stored locally on your device only.

4. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect any personal identification information.
  • We do not use analytics, crash reporting, or advertising SDKs.
  • We do not share any data with third parties (except AI API calls you explicitly configure).
  • We do not have any backend server — there is no ROHOR studio server that receives your data.

5. Third-Party AI Service (Google Gemini)

When you provide a Google Gemini API key and the App processes a video, the video URL or transcript is sent to Google's Gemini API for summarization. This is subject to Google's Privacy Policy. You control which API key is used and can remove it at any time in Settings.

6. Internet Access

The App accesses the internet solely for:

  • Fetching YouTube RSS feeds to check for new videos from channels you follow.
  • Fetching video transcripts from YouTube's public subtitle service.
  • Sending video data to the Google Gemini API for AI summarization.

7. Data Storage and Security

All data (API keys, video history, channel list, preferences) is stored locally on your device. API keys are stored using Android's EncryptedSharedPreferences (AES-256 encryption). No data leaves your device except AI API calls as described in Section 5.

8. Children's Privacy

This App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
