Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026 | Developer: ROHOR studio

1. Overview

YouTube Smart Notifications ("the App") is developed by ROHOR studio. This Privacy Policy explains what data the App accesses, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data. The App has no backend server of its own; all data is stored on your device, and data is transmitted only to the third-party services described below in order to provide the App's features. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

2. Notification Access (Notification Listener Permission)

The App requires the Notification Listener Service permission (android.permission.BIND_NOTIFICATION_LISTENER_SERVICE).

This permission is used solely to:

  • Detect when a new YouTube video upload notification arrives on your device.
  • Extract the video title and channel name from that notification in order to find and summarize the video.

We do not read, store, transmit, or process notifications from any other application. Only notifications originating from the official YouTube app are processed. Notification content is never sent to any third party other than the services explicitly described in Section 5.

3. Data We Collect and Process

The App processes the following data, stored locally on your device:

  • YouTube notification content — video title and channel name, used only to identify the video for summarization.
  • Video summaries — AI-generated summaries are stored locally in an on-device database (Room/SQLite).
  • API key — The Google Gemini API key you enter is stored securely on-device using Android's EncryptedSharedPreferences (AES-256). It is transmitted only to Google's Gemini API and to no one else.
  • Channel subscriptions — The list of YouTube channels you add or import is stored locally on your device only.

4. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect any personal identification information.
  • We do not use analytics, crash reporting, or advertising SDKs.
  • We do not operate any backend server — there is no ROHOR studio server that receives your data.
  • We do not sell your data, and we do not share it with any party other than the third-party services you use to operate the App (Section 5).

5. Third-Party Services

To provide its features, the App communicates directly with the following services from your device:

  • Google Gemini API — When you provide a Google Gemini API key and the App processes a video, the video reference (URL/ID) and/or its transcript and metadata are sent to Google's Gemini API to generate the summary. This is subject to Google's Privacy Policy. You control which API key is used and can remove it at any time in Settings.
  • YouTube (public services) — The App fetches publicly available data from YouTube to identify and analyze videos: channel RSS feeds (for new videos), public video pages, captions/subtitles, and video descriptions.
  • YouTube subscription import (Google Sign-In, optional) — If you choose to use "Import subscriptions", the App signs you in with your Google account and requests read-only access to your YouTube subscriptions (the https://www.googleapis.com/auth/youtube.readonly scope). Your subscription list is used only to populate the App's channel list on your device. It is not stored on any server and is not shared with anyone. You can revoke this access at any time at myaccount.google.com/permissions.

Use of YouTube data is also subject to the YouTube Terms of Service.

6. Google API Services — Limited Use

The App's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs (including data obtained via the youtube.readonly scope) adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including its Limited Use requirements. Specifically, data accessed from your Google account is used only to provide user-facing features within the App (populating your channel list), is processed on your device, is not transferred to others except as necessary to provide those features, and is never used for advertising.

7. Internet Access

The App accesses the internet solely for:

  • Fetching YouTube RSS feeds, video pages, captions, and descriptions to detect and analyze videos.
  • Sending video data to the Google Gemini API for AI summarization.
  • Importing your YouTube subscriptions via Google Sign-In, when you choose to do so.

8. Data Storage and Security

All data (API keys, video history, channel list, preferences) is stored locally on your device. API keys are stored using Android's EncryptedSharedPreferences (AES-256 encryption). No data leaves your device except the third-party calls described in Section 5.

9. Children's Privacy

This App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
